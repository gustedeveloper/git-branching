Chapter 2: The Prophecy Unveiled
Guided by Aurelius, Elara embarks on a journey through mystical realms, encountering beings of ancient wisdom who unveil a forgotten prophecy. The prophecy speaks of a time when Eldoria's magic would wane, and a chosen sorceress would emerge to retrieve the Elemental Crystals, scattered across the realm. Elara accepts her role, understanding the gravity of her quest and the significance of restoring Eldoria's true magical essence.
